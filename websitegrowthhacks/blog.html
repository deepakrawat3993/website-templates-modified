Why SEO is Important ?

SEO is important because it helps to increase rank on search engines. Users trust search engines, and achieving a top spot in search engine rankings signals to searchers that your site is a credible source. The higher you rank in results pages, the more clicks and traffic your site will generate.

June 17, 2013

How SEO is done ?

There are lots of ways that improves SEO results but some of the basic SEO techniques are:

1. Improve user experience across your entire site
2. Conquer video with YouTube SEO
3. Focus on topic clusters instead of keywords
4. Go into detail – but only when it’s relevant
5. Get a grip over technical optimization
6. Build a variety of backlinks
7. Optimize for voice search
8. Know how to measure SEO performance
9. Target local searchers with local landing pages and listings
